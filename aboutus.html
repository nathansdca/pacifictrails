Why Pacific Trails?

Pacific Trails was created as a luxury resort designed to accommodate your yearning for outdoor adventure. Our mission is to be an affordable and relaxing gateway to exploration, fun, and discovery while letting our knowledgeable staff do the hard work for you. Big Sur, where we are located, is a quaint and charming seaside town full of many things to do. You won't be disappointed when you come and visit us. More About Pacific Trails Resort

Big Sur is located 2 and a half hours from San Francisco and 5 hours from Los Angeles. Our remote location allows you to ease in to Nature and forget about your life's worries. The cozy Yurts are private and fully furnished, allowing you to relax in a serene environment.

The temperature year-round stays between the 60s and 70s, and at night is no colder than 50 degrees, allowing for many activities both day and night that suit your desires. Let our experienced guides lead you through beautiful hiking trails filled with flora and fauna or on a birdwatching trip. Kayak through the underwater kelp forests full of biodiversity. All gear is provided and instruction is available upon request.

Big Sur: More Things to Do

Big Sur has many amenities if you ever need something beyond what Pacific Trails Resort can offer. Here is a list of places that you may find helpful during your peaceful stay.

  • Whole Food's Market - get groceries and general food items on the corner of Main Street and 1st Avenue.
  • Salty Sea Bakery - sweet and savory pastries fill this family-owned shop.
  • Lighthouse Bistro - find your new breakfast favorite at this local coffee house.
  • Neptune's Diner - let the waiters do the work as you relax with your fancy seafood dinner overlooking the ocean.
  • Fernwood Tavern - grab a drink at the bar and have a chat with the friendly staff.
  • Big Sur Hospital - open 24/7 with an emergency room as well.
  • Path of Light Yoga Studio - all levels welcome and you can drop in anytime.
